Our Story

A Passion for Perfect Coffee

Founded in 2015, Sepintas Coffee began as a small dream to bring authentic coffee experiences to our community. Our name "Sepintas" reflects those fleeting yet memorable moments we create with every cup.

We source only the finest specialty-grade beans from sustainable farms around the world. Each batch is carefully roasted in-house to highlight the unique characteristics of the origin.
